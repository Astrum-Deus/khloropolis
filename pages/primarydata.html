The Expert's Perspective

We interviewed experts for their opinion on the integration of greenery into our cities.

U Than Htun

Founder of Ever Win Construction Co. Ltd.

U Than Htun's Perspective

This interview is about the current problems with Naypyitaw's greenery and agriculture, government plans and improvement the agricultural industry could make to enhance urban gardens and farm.

“The problem with our greenspaces is the lack of proper rulekeeping and maintenance.”

- U Than Htun, Founder of Ever Win Construction Co. Ltd.

U Wizaya Nay Kyaw

Manager of United TEC Construction PTE. Ltd.

U Wizaya Nay Kyaw’s Perspective

This interview discuss about sustainable solutions and ways to implement smart and sustainability into cities with urban gardens and innovative urban gardens like rooftop gardens. And it discuss about some of Singapore's urban gardens and rooftop gardens.

- U Wizaya Nay Kyaw, Manager of United TEC Construction PTE. Ltd.
